Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Overview

GhostSweep History ("the extension") is designed with a "Local-First" architecture. We do not transmit your browsing data to any external servers.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share your personal browsing history. All analysis (checking for keywords/titles) happens locally on your device.

3. License Keys

If you purchase the Pro version, your License Key is verified via the Gumroad API. No browsing data is sent during this process.
